The Route

From North to South, all the cities close to the trail or big cities with a (long) detour. If you know any friends living in these cities what might accept to give us a hand for the trip, it would be very nice. The help can be from receiving a parcel with spare parts, using the backyard as a camping, a stop for a proper lunch or a stop for a night  & shower :-) It will feel much better to see friends of friends than to stay at a motel.

Leave a comment on the page with your name and your email in the “email” field only, and we will contact you.

  • MONTANA - Eureka Whitefish Columbia Falls Swan Lake Seeley Lake Lincoln Helena Butte Wise River Bannack Lima Lakeview

  • IDAHO - Island Park

  • WYOMING - Colter Bay Village Moran Jackson (detour) Cora Pinedale Boulder Big Sandy Rawlins Sinclair

  • COLORADO - Columbine Steamboat Springs Kremmling Denver (detour) Silverthorne Dillon Jefferson Hartsel Colorado Springs (detour) Salida Sargents Doyleville La Garita Del Norte South Fork

  • NEW MEXICO - El Rito Abiquiu Medanales Espanola (detour) Cuba Torreon Ambrosia Lake San Mateo Grants Albuquerque (detour) Old Horse Springs Fierro Santa Clara Silver City Tyrone Separ Hachita Antelope Wells - END!
