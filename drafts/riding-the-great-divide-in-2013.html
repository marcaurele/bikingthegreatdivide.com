Riding the Great Divide in 2013?

Mon 27 May 2013
Marc-Aurèle

[][]If you are planning on riding the Great Divide this summer in 2013, and you have a Spot device, I will enormously appreciate if you could drop me a quick email to project@bikingthegreatdivide.com with your “viewspots” page (something like this one: http://share.findmespot.com/shared/faces/viewspots.jsp?glId=0ZjkbYIYk85IZfZBouliXmzfTUWOvPa2T) so that I can have more test data to build the community website I want to launch for all riders like you. I will only use your spot points to test my pages, and will trash them when the site will be live.

Thank you!
